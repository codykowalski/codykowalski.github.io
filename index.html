Cody Kowalski

Maker of Violin,
Viola & Cello Bows

I am a contemporary bow maker working in the French style. I completed my first bow at age thirteen under the guidance of Charles Espey in Port Townsend, WA, and continued studying with him throughout my high school years. Since then, I have made bows for musicians from across the US. Take a look at examples of my recent work below, and call or email if you have any questions.
